What’s your story, guy?

“Interesting question. What is my story? I can’t exactly tell you. I don’t have any memories of my old life. Guess it wasn’t that interesting if I can’t remember. I can’t even remember my name."

"They call me Resident 39, but my pals call me Burrito.”

Who are your pals?

“I do not have any. I don’t leave this room. It’s more cozy to lay here in this big roll and watch the imagination in my head. I’d turn the TV on, but… ya know… cozy. I could sleep here all day. But… I do.”
